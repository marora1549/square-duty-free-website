Our Legacy

Crafting exceptional experiences for discerning connoisseurs

A Vision for Excellence

At Square Duty Free, we take pride in being the premier destination for liquor and tobacco connoisseurs visiting the beautiful Caribbean islands, especially Saint Kitts. Since our establishment in 2005, we have been dedicated to providing an exceptional shopping experience for tourists seeking the finest selection of premium international wines, liquors, and tobacco products.

With a passion for quality and a commitment to customer satisfaction, we have become a trusted name in the industry.

Uncompromising Quality

We are committed to providing only the highest quality liquor and tobacco products. Each item in our collection undergoes rigorous selection criteria to ensure authenticity, excellence, and unparalleled taste.

Women & Tourist Friendly

Your safety and comfort are of utmost importance to us. Our stores are designed to provide a welcoming and secure environment for all our customers, including women and tourists. We go above and beyond to ensure that our customers are satisfied.

Meet the Visionary

The entrepreneurial spirit behind Square Duty Free

Sunny Gehani

Entrepreneur & Philanthropist

Sunil Gehani, popularly known as Sunny Gehani, is a prominent figure in the business community, known for his entrepreneurial spirit, philanthropy, and success across different industries. Born and raised in Mumbai, he moved to Saint Martin in 1998 in search of work and started a business of vending machines.

Despite the challenges, he persevered and went on to establish a duty-free tobacco & liquor store in 2002. Besides owning over 4 liquor stores at Robert L. Bradshaw International Airport, he owns liquor brands and engages in liquor production locally.

Apart from the liquor industry, Sunny is also involved in other businesses, including the Big Cane Company and St. Kitts Cigar Company, a cigar manufacturing company that produces hand-rolled cigars using traditional methods. He is also the founder and CEO of Global Link International, an immigration consultancy service company.

Sunny's dedication to entrepreneurship is only matched by his commitment to community service. He has supported various charitable initiatives that include education and healthcare projects in India. His story is a true testament to the power of hard work and determination.
